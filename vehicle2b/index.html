Vehicle2b

Vehicle 2b exhibits positive phototaxis, or directed movement towards a light. Light on one side of the robot makes the motor on the opposite side to run faster causing it to turn towards the light. This is achieved by crossing the nerve fibres.

See Braitenbot #2: Fear and Aggression for more info.

Differential Steering based on Rossum Elementary Trajectory Model
