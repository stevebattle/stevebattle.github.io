BraitenBots

A Braitenberg vehicle is a robot that can autonomously move around based on its sensor inputs. It has primitive sensors that measure some stimulus at a point, and one or two wheels. In the simplest configuration, a sensor is directly connected to an effector, so that a sensed signal immediately produces a movement of the wheel.
Vehicle 1: Getting Around
Braitenberg Vehicle 1 is stimulated by light, making it move faster as it approaches the light source.
Vehicle 2: Fight or Flight
Braitenberg Vehicle 2 exhibits phototaxis, movement towards or away from, a light source.
